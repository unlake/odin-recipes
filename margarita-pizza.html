Margaritapizza

DESCRIPTION

This is easily the best margaritapizza what you have eaten.
Italian style margaritapizza which keeps you full whole day.

Ingredients

  • Flour 4dl
  • Warm water 2dl
  • Yeast 25g
  • Tomatosauce
  • Pizza cheese
  • Mozarella
  1. Mix yeast with warm water and add flour. Knead dough and make it like the bottom of the pizza.
  2. Take tomatosauce and spread it on top of the pizzabottom. After that take pizzacheese an mozarella an tune it over the tomatosauce.
  3. Put pizza to owen at 200C for 1/2 hour.
