Tunapasta

DESCRIPTION

This is easily the best tunapasta what you have eaten.
Tunapasta can be tuned with various different ingredients and spices.

Ingredients

  • 2 tuna cans
  • Pasta of your choice
  • Cucumber
  • Cream cheese (herb)
  • Curry
  1. Boil the pasta and cut cucumber to tiny cubes
  2. After pasta is cooked add tuna and cream cheese to pasta
  3. Mix the ingredients at kettle and spice with curry or other spices. You are ready to enjoy!
